Your privacy matters. Royale Block is designed to be a fun, private experience — we do not collect, sell, or share your personal data with third parties.

This Privacy Policy explains how Royale Block ("the Game", "we", "our") handles information when you play. By using the Game, you agree to the practices described here.

01 —

Information We Collect

Royale Block operates entirely on your device. We collect only the minimum data required for the game to function:

  • Game Progress & High Scores — stored locally on your device via localStorage, never transmitted externally.
  • Settings Preferences — sound, display, and gameplay preferences saved locally.
  • Tutorial Status — a flag indicating whether you have completed the first-time tutorial, stored locally.

We do not collect your name, email address, location, device identifiers, or any personally identifiable information.

02 —

How We Use Information

The locally stored data is used exclusively to:

  • Save and display your best scores across sessions.
  • Remember your in-game settings and preferences.
  • Track whether the first-play tutorial has been shown.

This data never leaves your device and is never processed by any server or third party.

03 —

Data Storage

All game data is stored using your browser's localStorage. This means:

  • Data remains on your device only.
  • Clearing your browser data or cache will erase all saved game data.
  • No backups are made to any external server.
You are in full control of your data. Delete it at any time by clearing your browser's local storage or site data.
04 —

Third-Party Services

Royale Block does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising networks, social SDKs, or tracking tools.

The game loads fonts from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com) to display its typography. This request may expose your IP address to Google under their own Privacy Policy. No personal data from within the game is shared in this request.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information to any third party — ever.

05 —

Children's Privacy

Royale Block is a family-friendly puzzle game suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction).

Since we collect no personal data whatsoever, no special procedures are needed to protect children's privacy beyond the general practices described in this policy.

If you are a parent or guardian and have concerns, please contact us at the details in Section 9.

06 —

Your Rights

Because we do not collect personal data, most privacy rights (access, portability, correction, erasure) are fully under your own control:

  • Access & Deletion — view or delete your game data at any time via your browser's developer tools or settings.
  • No Account Required — there is no account, profile, or registration in Royale Block.
  • Opt-Out — simply stop playing or clear your browser data at any time.
07 —

Data Security

Since all game data resides on your own device and is never transmitted over a network, the security of that data is subject to your device and browser security settings.

We recommend keeping your browser and operating system up to date for the best security posture. We cannot be held responsible for data loss or corruption arising from browser storage limitations or device issues.

08 —

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the game or applicable law. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Continued use of the game after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
